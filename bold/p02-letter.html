A Note Before You Begin
A Letter to
Your Brain
Dear Brain,

You are not broken. You never were.

Traditional planners were not built for you. They assume you'll feel time passing, remember what you wrote three days ago, and start tasks without needing a running start.

You know that's not how this works — and that's okay. That's not a failure. That's just the shape of your brain.

This planner was built differently. Every page exists because your brain has a specific, real challenge — and there's a specific, real tool to meet it. Time blocks for time blindness. Brain dumps for working memory. Energy forecasts for the days you can't predict yourself.

You don't have to use every page. You don't have to start on a Monday. You don't have to be consistent to belong here.

You just have to show up when you can.
That will always be enough.
— Brain OS
